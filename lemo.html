Website Lemo

As a school project in 2017 we (a team of 4 students) got a project assigned to develop an informative website for Lemo. Lemo is a test to see how much motivation students have when they start college and it gives them tips about how they can improve that. This site had to have a CMS (we used wordpress), and it had to be divided by a portal in different classes. It was a big task but we managed to get it done!
